# Stripe [Cards, bank transfers, and invoices]

:::warning[Coming soon]
Stripe support is still in beta. Follow @stripe and @tempo on Twitter to learn when it goes live.
:::

The Stripe payment method enables traditional payment rails through [Stripe](https://stripe.com)— including credit cards, bank transfers, and invoices.

## Intents

| Intent | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `charge` | One-time card or bank payment |

## Build your own

Need Stripe payments now? See [Custom payment methods](/payment-methods/custom) for a guide on implementing your own Stripe integration with the mpay SDK.
